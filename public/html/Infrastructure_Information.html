IIDSC

British Specialised Centre in Infrastructure Consultation

IIDSC provides expert consultations in key areas including establishing national centres, offering advanced geomatics and BIM solutions, and delivering targeted training programs in Facilities and Asset Management (FAM) and related fields.

Establishment of Dedicated National Centres

IIDSC focuses on establishing dedicated National Centres in Middle Eastern countries, providing state-of-the-art geomatics and BIM solutions for urban infrastructure and superstructures management.

Urban City Infrastructure Solutions

Considering specific infrastructure elements within urban cities, IIDSC's solutions offer the following benefits:

  • • Organize all information about infrastructure in urban cities.
  • • Detect malfunctioning infrastructure elements efficiently.
  • • Generate a five-year development plan with a click of a button.
  • • Manage infrastructures effectively across institutions.
  • • Obtain multiple scenario solutions for development, planning, and maintenance programs with ease.
IIDSC: Making the Engineer's Dream a Reality

This vision aligns with the aspirations of engineers, decision-makers, planners, and politicians, making complex urban infrastructure management more intelligent, efficient, and actionable.
